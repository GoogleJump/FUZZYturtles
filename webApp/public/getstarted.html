This webapp helps you split a bill when you dine with friends!

To get started: Navigate to the Home or Advanced Features page

  1. From the home page:
    • Enter the total bill, number of guests, and desired tip percentage, and our app will split the bill evenly among all members of the party.
  2. From the Advanced Features page:
    • Enter the name of each guest, along with their respective amounts
    • Enter a tax amount, or leave blank
    • Select a tip amount: 15%, 18%, 20%, or enter your own tip percentage
